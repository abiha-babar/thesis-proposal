\section{Project Plan}
\label{sec:plan}

The table below shows the step-by-step plan for completing this thesis. Each stage shows what reading and writing will be done, alongside what practical work needs to happen at the same time.

\begin{table}[h!]
    \centering
    \begin{tabularx}{\textwidth}{ | c | X | X | }
        \hline
        \textbf{Time} & \textbf{Reading / Writing} & \textbf{Practical Work} \\
        \hline
        Weeks 1--4   & Read all related research papers and finish the literature review & Choose which three local AI models and which online AI service will be used; check hardware needed for each \\
        \hline
        Weeks 5--6   & Plan and write up how the dataset will be built & Create 500 test documents and prepare a complete answer key for every field \\
        \hline
        Weeks 7--9   & Write the methodology chapter & Set up all three local models on the test laptop; set up energy logging (\texttt{powermetrics} or \texttt{powertop}); connect to the online AI service and turn on billing tracking; write and test the three instruction versions \\
        \hline
        Weeks 10--13 & Write up how the experiment was designed & Run all tests: send all 500 documents to all four systems using all three instruction versions, and record every result \\
        \hline
        Weeks 14--16 & Write the results chapter & Check accuracy scores for each instruction version; measure speed; calculate electricity cost and hardware share per document; find the breakeven point for each local model \\
        \hline
        Weeks 17--18 & Write the discussion and conclusion chapters & Create charts and tables showing accuracy by instruction version, speed, cost per document, and breakeven curves \\
        \hline
        Weeks 19--20 & Send the first complete draft to the supervisor for feedback & Do a final check of all data and figures \\
        \hline
        Weeks 21--22 & Make all corrections based on feedback & Submit the final completed thesis \\
        \hline
    \end{tabularx}
    \caption{Planned Project Timeline}
    \label{tab:timeline}
\end{table}

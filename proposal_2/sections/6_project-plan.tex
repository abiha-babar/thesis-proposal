\section{Project Plan}
\label{sec:plan}

The table below outlines the planned timeline for completing this thesis. Reading, writing, and practical implementation activities proceed in parallel throughout the project to ensure that empirical work informs the written chapters in real time.

\begin{table}[h!]
    \centering
    \begin{tabularx}{\textwidth}{ | c | X | X | }
        \hline
        \textbf{Time} & \textbf{Reading / Writing} & \textbf{Practical Work} \\
        \hline
        Weeks 1--3   & Read foundational papers on tool use, agentic skills, and trajectory evaluation (Toolformer, Gorilla, AgentBench, ReAct, WebArena, and the context saturation literature) & Install and configure Ollama; download and verify \texttt{gemma4:4b} and \texttt{qwen3.5:4b}; obtain API access for Gemini~3.1~Pro and GPT-4o; clone and install OpenClaw \\
        \hline
        Weeks 4--5   & Draft and finalise the literature review chapter & Design the 20-skill library structure; write the SKILL.md template; define applicability conditions, execution policies, and termination criteria for all three skill domains \\
        \hline
        Weeks 6--7   & Draft the thesis introduction and finalise the three research questions & Implement all 20 SKILL.md files; build and validate the equivalent Condition~A flat JSON schema representations; verify that both conditions execute correctly end-to-end \\
        \hline
        Weeks 8--9   & Draft the methodology chapter & Construct the full 40-task benchmark; prepare ground-truth answer keys and policy graphs for trajectory compliance scoring; implement the Python orchestration controller and all logging infrastructure \\
        \hline
        Weeks 10--12 & Document experimental setup and measurement instrumentation & Execute all experiments: 40 tasks $\times$ 2 conditions $\times$ 4 model backends $\times$ 3 repetitions = 960 agent runs; record all token counts, latency measurements, energy readings, and action traces \\
        \hline
        Weeks 13--15 & Draft the results chapter & Compute all metrics; generate comparative tables and charts covering task completion rate, hallucination count, trajectory compliance, token cost, latency, and inference cost \\
        \hline
        Weeks 16--17 & Draft the discussion and conclusion chapters & Conduct sensitivity checks (alternative task ordering and prompt phrasing); verify that findings are stable across repetitions \\
        \hline
        Weeks 18--19 & Compile the first complete draft and submit to supervisor for feedback & Finalise all appendices: Python source code, SKILL.md files, task benchmark, raw data tables \\
        \hline
        Weeks 20--22 & Revise all chapters in response to supervisor feedback & Prepare and submit the final corrected thesis \\
        \hline
    \end{tabularx}
    \caption{Planned Project Timeline}
    \label{tab:timeline}
\end{table}

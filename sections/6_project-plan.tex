\section{Project Plan}
\label{sec:plan}

The table below shows the step-by-step plan for completing this thesis. Each stage shows what reading and writing will be done, alongside what practical work needs to happen at the same time.

\begin{table}[h!]
    \centering
    \begin{tabularx}{\textwidth}{ | c | X | X | }
        \hline
        \textbf{Time} & \textbf{Reading / Writing} & \textbf{Practical Work} \\
        \hline
        Weeks 1--4   & Read all related research papers and finish the literature review & Decide which local AI tool and which online AI service will be used in the test \\
        \hline
        Weeks 5--6   & Plan and document how the dataset will be structured & Create 500 test documents with a complete answer key for every field \\
        \hline
        Weeks 7--9   & Write the methodology chapter & Set up the local AI tool on the laptop; connect to the online AI service and set up billing tracking \\
        \hline
        Weeks 10--13 & Write up how the experiment was designed & Run all tests. Send all 500 documents to both AI options and record all results \\
        \hline
        Weeks 14--16 & Write the results chapter & Check accuracy scores, measure speed, calculate cost per document, and find the breakeven point \\
        \hline
        Weeks 17--18 & Write the discussion and conclusion chapters & Create charts and tables to present the results clearly \\
        \hline
        Weeks 19--20 & Send the first complete draft to the supervisor for feedback & Do a final check of all data and figures \\
        \hline
        Weeks 21--22 & Make all corrections based on feedback & Submit the final completed thesis \\
        \hline
    \end{tabularx}
    \caption{Planned Project Timeline}
    \label{tab:timeline}
\end{table}
